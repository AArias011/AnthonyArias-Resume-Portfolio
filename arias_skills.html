My Skills

    Active Listening:

    I give full attention to what other people are saying, I take time to understand the points being made, I ask questions as appropriate, and I am not interrupting at inappropriate times.

    Time Management:

    I manage my own time and the time of others.

    Active Learning:

    I understand the implications of new information for both current and future problem-solving and decision-making.

    Critical Thinking:

    I use logic and reasoning to identify the strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, conclusions or approaches to problems.

    Judgment and Decision Making:

    I consider the relative costs and benefits of potential actions to choose the most appropriate one.
